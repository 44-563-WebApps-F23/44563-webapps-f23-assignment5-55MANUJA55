Pune

Pune is the second-largest city in the state of Maharashtra after Mumbai, and is an important city in terms of its economic and industrial growth. Once the centre of power for the Maratha Empire, Pune's rich historical past has made it the cultural capital of Maharashtra.

Rajpur

Rajpur is one of the popular locality in the Northern part of Dehradun, Uttarakhand. It has an excellent connectivity with other parts of the city through Rajpur Road. Dehradun Train Junction and the Jolly Grant Airport is at a short driving distance from this area.

Ajmer

Ajmer is an oasis wrapped in the green hills. The city was founded by Raja Ajay Pal Chauhan in the 7th Century A.D. and continued to be a major centre of the Chauhan power till 1193 A.D. When Prithviraj Chauhan lost it to Mohammed Gaure. Since then, Ajmer became home to many dynasties.
